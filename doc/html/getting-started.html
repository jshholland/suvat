Getting started

Once Suvat is installed, run it by typing suvat at the command prompt. The Suvat window will launch and present you with 5 entry boxes and two buttons. The interface is pretty forgiving, and you can probably work out what’s going on by experimentation.

If you’re unsure of what to do, try entering 3 numbers into the entry boxes and hit “calculate”. If the numbers make sense in terms of the motion of an object, the other boxes will be filled in with the other details of the movement of the object. Otherwise, a popup will appear and tell you that the numbers you entered are invalid.
